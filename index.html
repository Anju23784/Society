RAHNUMA

RAHNUMA is the official dramatics society of Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women. The name of the society is derived from an Urdu word, which means “the one who guides”. Foundations of our society were laid by some very talented and driven students of this college on 16th November 2013, when Indira Gandhi Institute of Technology became Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women. In the past 6 working years of this society, we have won numerous street play competitions and our productions were acclaimed by masses. We have also given many public performances on various occasions.
